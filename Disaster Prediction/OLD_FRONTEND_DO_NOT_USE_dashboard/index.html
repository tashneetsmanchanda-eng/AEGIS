About DisasterPredict

DisasterPredict is an AI-powered risk assessment system that predicts the likelihood of disease outbreaks following natural disasters like floods .

How It Works

1

Input Conditions

Enter environmental factors like monsoon intensity, infrastructure quality, and preparedness levels.

2

Flood Prediction

Our ML model predicts flood probability using ensemble methods (Random Forest, Gradient Boosting).

3

Disease Correlation

Based on flood severity and conditions, we predict risks for malaria, cholera, leptospirosis, and hepatitis.
